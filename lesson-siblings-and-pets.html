Transcription

Part 01

Spruce: Hey Maple, do you have siblings?

Maple: Yes, I have a brother. Do you have siblings?

Spruce: No, I don't have a brother or a sister. I am an only child.

Maple: Oh, I see.

A: - - -

Part 02

Maple: Does your family have a pet?

Spruce: Yes, we have a dog. His name is Max. Do you have a pet?

Maple: No, I don't have a pet, but my cousin has a cat.

Spruce: That's nice!

A: - - -

Part 03

Spruce: Does your brother have a pet?

Maple: No, he doesn't have a pet, but he wants a dog.

Spruce: Dogs are great! My neighbors have two cats. They love animals.

Maple: That's cool!

A: - - -

Part 04

Maple: Do they have any other pets?

Spruce: No, they don't have other pets. But they want a dog too.

Maple: Pets are wonderful!

Spruce: Yes, they are!

A: - - -
