Transcription

Part 01

Maple: Hi Cove! Hi Spruce! How are you today?

Cove: Hey Maple! I'm great, thanks. How about you, Spruce?

Spruce: I'm doing well, thanks.

Part 02

Maple: Cove, what is your favorite food?

Cove: I love pizza! How about you, Spruce?

Spruce: I like sushi the most.

Part 03

Cove: Spruce, what season do you like?

Spruce: I enjoy summer. Maple, how about you?

Maple: I prefer winter. It's so cozy!

Part 04

Spruce: Maple, do you dislike any colours?

Maple: I really don't like yellow. Cove, what about you?

Cove: I'm not a fan of orange.

Part 05

Maple: Cove, who is your favorite singer?

Cove: My favorite singer is Taylor Swift. Spruce, who do you like?

Spruce: I admire Beyoncé.

Part 06

Cove: Spruce, who is your favorite actor?

Spruce: I really like Leonardo DiCaprio. Maple, who is your favourite actor?

Maple: I'm a big fan of Emma Watson.
