Transcription

Part 01

Cove: Hi, Maple! How are you?

Maple: Hi, Cove! I am good. How about you?

Cove: I am good too. Look! This is my brother. He is a student.

Maple: Oh, nice! And your sister? Is she a student too?

Cove: No, she is a doctor.

Maple: That is interesting!

Part 02

Maple: My father is a teacher. My mother is a manager.

Cove: Wow! They are busy!

Maple: Yes, they are. My cousin is a reporter.

Cove: Really? That is cool!

Maple: Yes, it is.

Part 03

Maple: What about your family?

Cove: My cousin and I are students. My uncle is a doctor.

Maple: That is nice! We have big families!

Cove: Yes, we do!
