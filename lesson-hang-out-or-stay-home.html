Transcription

Part 01

Maple: Hey Vale, hey Ember! What are you going to do on Monday?

Vale: Hi Maple! On Monday, I am going to work in the morning and study in the evening. What about you, Ember?

Ember: I will stay home on Monday. I'm tired! But on Tuesday, I am going to go out with my cousin.

Maple: Nice!

A: - - -

Part 02

Maple: I will study on Monday and Tuesday. I have a big test!

Vale: What about Wednesday? I am going to hang out with some friends after work.

Ember: Oh cool! I will go out on Wednesday night too. Maple, do you want to come?

Maple: Yes, I do! It will be fun!

A: - - -

Part 03

Vale: What are you going to do on Thursday, Ember?

Ember: On Thursday, I will work all day. But on Friday, I am going to stay home and relax.

Maple: I will go out on Friday. It's my brother's birthday!

Vale: And Saturday?

Maple: I am going to work in the morning, but I will stay home in the evening.

A: - - -

Part 04

Ember: I will hang out with my sister on Saturday.

Vale: Cool! On Sunday, I am going to go out with my family. We will eat pizza!

Maple: Yum! Sunday sounds fun.
