Transcription

Part 01

Spruce: Hey Maple, do you like chocolate?

Maple: Yes, I like chocolate! Do you like chocolate too?

Spruce: Yes, I do! I also like pizza. Do you like pizza?

Maple: Yes, I like pizza a lot!

Part 02

Maple: Do you like coffee?

Spruce: No, I don't like coffee, but I like tea. Do you like tea?

Maple: Yes, I do! Tea is good. Does your brother like coffee?

Spruce: No, he doesn't like coffee, but he likes tea too.

Maple: That's nice!

Part 03

Maple: Does your sister like beetroot?

Spruce: No, she doesn't like beetroot. But she likes scarlet eggplants.

Maple: I don't like scarlet eggplants. Do your parents like scarlet eggplants?

Spruce: Yes, they like scarlet eggplants, but they don't like beetroot.

Maple: My parents like both!

Spruce: That's interesting!
