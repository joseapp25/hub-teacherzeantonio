Transcription

Part 01

Spruce: Hey Maple, does your brother have breakfast in the morning?

Maple: Yes, he has breakfast every day. Does your sister have coffee in the morning?

Spruce: No, she doesn't have coffee. She drinks tea.

Maple: That's nice!

Part 02

Maple: Does your father eat vegetables for lunch?

Spruce: Yes, he eats vegetables every day. Does your mother have tea in the evening?

Maple: No, she doesn't have tea. She drinks juice.

Part 03

Spruce: My parents have dinner at 7 p.m. Does your family have dinner early?

Maple: No, we have dinner late. My father eats pizza at night.

Spruce: That sounds delicious!

Part 04

Spruce: Does your sister have fruit for dessert?

Maple: Yes, she has fruit after lunch. Does your brother drink milk?

Spruce: No, he doesn't drink milk. He has juice in the morning.

Maple: Juice is a good choice!
