Part 01

Maple: What is red?

Vale: Apples are red. Fire trucks are red.

Maple: What is green?

Vale: Leaves are green. Grass is green.

Part 02

Vale: What is blue?

Maple: The ocean is blue. The sky is blue.

Vale: What is brown?

Maple: Chocolate is brown. Some dogs are brown.

A: - - -

Part 03

Maple: What is orange?
