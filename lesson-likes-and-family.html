Transcription

Part 01

Arbor: Hey Maple, do you have a brother or a sister?
Maple: Yes, I have a sister. Do you have siblings?
Arbor: No, I don't have a brother or a sister, but I have a dog.
Maple: Oh, that's nice! I like dogs.

Part 02

Maple: Do you like cats too?
Arbor: No, I don't like cats, but I like chocolate and pizza. What about you?
Maple: I love pizza! But I don't like chocolate.
Arbor: Really? That's interesting.

Part 03

Arbor: Does your sister like chocolate?
Maple: Yes, she does! She loves chocolate and tea.
Arbor: Cool! Does she have a pet?
Maple: No, she doesn't have a pet. But we have a cat at home.
Arbor: That's nice.

Part 04

Arbor: Do your parents like coffee or tea?
Maple: My mom likes coffee, but my dad likes tea. What about your parents?
Arbor: They like coffee. They drink it every morning.
Maple: Coffee is great! Do we have time to get some now? Arbor: Yes, let's go!
