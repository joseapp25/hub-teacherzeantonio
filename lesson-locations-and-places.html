Transcription

Part 01

Vale: Hi Maple! How are you?

Maple: Hi Vale! I'm good, thanks. How about you?

Vale: I'm great, thanks for asking. Where are you right now?

Maple: I'm at home. Where are you?

Vale: I'm at the office.

Part 02

Vale: What do you do at home?

Maple: I'm studying for my exams. What do you do at the office?

Vale: I am working on a project with my team.

Part 03

Vale: Where is your brother?

Maple: He is at school. Where is your sister?

Vale: She is at the supermarket. Are your parents at home?

Maple: No, they are at the mall. Are your cousins at the park?

Vale: Yes, they are playing soccer there.

Part 04

Vale: Do you go to the beach with your family?

Maple: Yes, we go every summer. Do you like university?

Vale: Yes, I do. I am learning a lot there.

Maple: That's great! I am happy to hear that.
