Transcription

Part 01

Maple: Hi, Vale! Are you in a big city?

Vale: Yes, Maple. I am in New York. It is a big and loud city. It is also a little dangerous.

Cove: Is it beautiful, Vale?

Vale: Yes, it is beautiful, but it is very expensive.

Part 02

Spruce: My city is small. It is quiet, cheap, and very safe.

Maple: Where are you from, Spruce?

Spruce: I am from a small town in Canada.

Cove: Is your town cold?

Spruce: Yes, it is cold. But it is very clear and clean.

Part 03

Maple: I am from Lisbon. It is hot, near the beach, and very beautiful.

Vale: Is it expensive, Maple?

Maple: No, it is not very expensive. But some places are dirty and dangerous.

Part 04

Cove: I am not in a big city. I am on a farm. It is far from the city and very safe.

Spruce: Are the people nice there, Cove?

Cove: Yes, they are nice and happy.

Part 05

Vale: Are you all in different places?

Maple: Yes, we are in different cities.

Spruce: They are different, but they are all interesting!
